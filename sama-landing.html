The Problem

India built a grid for an era that is ending.

300 GW of centralised coal and hydro. One direction. Predictable. Controllable. Then seventy million rooftops changed the equation, and the grid has no intelligence to manage what follows.

Generation

India added 150 GW of rooftop and distributed solar in five years. Surplus exports at a floor price set by the DISCOM, with no market mechanism and no consumer choice.

One direction. One price. No negotiation.
Data

Smart meter readings are collected but siloed inside DISCOM billing systems. Inverter data lives in third-party apps. No prosumer has a unified view of their own generation asset.

The data exists. The intelligence does not.
Markets

KERC P2P regulations are live. Smart meters are deploying. Beckn Protocol handles settlement. The only thing missing is a coordination layer between prosumers.

That layer is what Sama builds.
What we are building

Why Now

India's energy stack is being rewired in public.

Regulation
KERC P2P regulations live since August 2024
Karnataka domestic prosumers can sell directly to neighbours. DISCOMs must empanel approved service providers. The legal basis for Sama in Bengaluru.
KERC Implementation Regulations, 2024
Infrastructure
India Energy Stack launched June 2025
UPI-style open protocol for energy, led by Nandan Nilekani. Beckn handles matching, UPI handles settlement. The coordination layer now exists.
Ministry of Power, June 2025
Hardware
250 million smart meters deploying under RDSS
₹3 lakh crore programme creating the physical data layer for P2P settlement. Already active in Karnataka, Delhi, and UP — the same states with P2P regulations.
RDSS Scheme, MoP
Market

The Thesis

The next utility company in India will not look like a utility company.

Energy is the last consumer vertical in India that hasn't had its DPI moment. The regulation is there. The infrastructure is being built. What's missing is a consumer-facing intelligence layer that makes the invisible visible: your rooftop as an asset, your surplus as a position, your building as a node in a distributed grid.

Sama begins with the simplest wedge: show prosumers what they're losing. Build trust through transparency. Then, when the trades begin, become the marketplace — and above it, the aggregation and dispatch layer that turns ten million rooftops into a coordinated virtual power plant. The consumer app is the wedge. The data is the moat. The VPP is the business.

— The Sama Team, Bengaluru, 2026
