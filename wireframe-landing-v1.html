S4 — Why now (regulatory legitimacy)

This is legal. The framework is live.

P2P energy trading is now regulated in Karnataka, Delhi, and UP. The infrastructure is being built.

Karnataka
KERC P2P Regulations 2024
Effective August 6, 2024. Covers domestic consumers and solar prosumers. DISCOMs must empanel service providers.
National
India Energy Stack (IES)
DPI for the power sector — like UPI but for energy. Launched June 2025. Led by Nandan Nilekani. Open settlement rails for P2P transactions.
PM Note — Regulatory section The #1 objection from a prosumer will be "is this even legal?" We answer it before they ask. KERC cite is the most specific signal — it has a date and a jurisdiction. IES / Nandan Nilekani lends credibility to the national infrastructure story. Don't mention blockchain on the landing page — it reads as speculative to a non-technical audience.
