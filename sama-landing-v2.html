Problem

India's prosumers are generating clean energy into a market that cannot see them.

Forecasting is impossible

Rooftop solar generation varies by cloud, season, and monsoon. No existing tool gives a prosumer 24-hour ahead visibility into what they will generate, or what that will be worth.

The market pays nothing

Surplus exports at ₹2.50–4/unit — DISCOM's feed-in tariff. The market rate for the same unit is ₹6.80. The prosumer has no mechanism to access the difference.

DISCOMs have no visibility either

Distribution companies manage a grid increasingly full of two-way flows from rooftop solar. Their forecasting tools were built for one-way centralised generation.

The software layer does not exist

India Energy Stack launched. KERC P2P regulations are live. 250 million smart meters are deploying. The hardware is arriving. The intelligence layer has not been built.

Technology

We use machine learning to give the grid a nervous system.
