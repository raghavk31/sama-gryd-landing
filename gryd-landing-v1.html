Regulatory landscape

The legal basis exists.
The infrastructure is live.

P2P trading is a regulated activity in Karnataka, Delhi, and UP. Not speculative — the law is already in effect.

LIVE
KERC P2P Regulations 2024
Karnataka · 06.08.2024
Covers domestic consumers and SRTPV prosumers. DISCOMs must empanel approved service providers. The legal basis for Gryd in Bengaluru.
LIVE
India Energy Stack (IES)
National · 06.2025
DPI for the power sector led by Nandan Nilekani. Open settlement rails via Beckn Protocol and UEI. The UPI moment for energy in India.
ACTIVE
PM Surya Ghar Yojana
National · 2024–present
47.3 lakh applications, 10 lakh installations as of March 2026. The prosumer base is growing — and so is surplus going to waste at ₹2.50.
ACTIVE
RDSS Smart Meter Rollout
National · 250M meters
Physical settlement layer for P2P. Live in Karnataka, Delhi, and UP — the same three states with active P2P energy regulations.
